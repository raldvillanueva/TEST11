Help Center

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about SugarLoom Ph

How long does delivery take? +
We do same day delivery depending on your location within Manila.
How do I reheat my cookies? +
Warm in the oven for 5–8 minutes at low heat, or microwave for 15–20 seconds from room temperature for that fresh-baked feel.
Do you do custom orders? +
Yes! We love doing custom order boxes depending on your likings.
How long do cookies stay fresh? +
Our cookies stay fresh for up to 5 days at room temperature in an airtight container, or up to 2 weeks in the freezer.
What are your working hours? +
We're available Monday to Saturday, 8:00 AM – 5:00 PM. Orders and inquiries outside these hours will be attended to the next business day.
